Vivaio is a shopping and farming management game where the player has to build a farm and a shop to sell the products of the farm.
This game is a two-people project with only one programmer (me) and one artist. We're corrently working on it and publishing a new version every month. We work on it at the College Facilities (UJI's Espaitec) thanks to the INAT GameLab using Scrum methodologies.
